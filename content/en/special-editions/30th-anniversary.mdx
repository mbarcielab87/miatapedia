# 30th Anniversary Edition (2019)

*Celebrating three decades of pure driving pleasure*

The 30th Anniversary Edition marked a milestone in MX-5 history, celebrating 30 years since the original NA's debut. Limited to just 3,000 units worldwide, this special edition combined exclusive styling with performance enhancements to create one of the most desirable modern MX-5 variants.

## Overview

Launched in 2019, the 30th Anniversary Edition was based on the ND2 generation MX-5 and featured the uprated 2.0L Skyactiv-G engine producing 181 hp. Each car was individually numbered and came with exclusive Racing Orange paint, Rays wheels, and premium interior appointments.

**Key Specifications:**
- Production: 2019 (Single model year)
- Global production: 3,000 units
- Engine: 2.0L Skyactiv-G (181 hp)
- Transmission: 6-speed manual only
- Base price: $34,995 USD

## Exclusive Features

### Exterior
- **Racing Orange Paint:** Exclusive color not available on regular MX-5s
- **Rays Forged Aluminum Wheels:** 17" x 7" lightweight wheels (saving 8.8 lbs)
- **30th Anniversary Badges:** Special edition badging throughout
- **Orange Brake Calipers:** Color-matched Brembo calipers
- **Orange Mirror Caps:** Matching exterior accents

### Interior
- **Tan Leather Seats:** Premium Nappa leather with orange stitching
- **Orange Interior Accents:** Dashboard, door panels, and center console
- **Alcantara Steering Wheel:** With orange center stripe
- **30th Anniversary Floor Mats:** Embroidered special edition mats
- **Numbered Plaque:** Individual production number

## Performance Enhancements

### Suspension & Handling
- **Bilstein Dampers:** Sport-tuned suspension setup
- **Strut Tower Brace:** Enhanced chassis rigidity
- **Performance Tires:** Bridgestone Potenza S001 (205/45R17)

### Powertrain
- **2.0L Skyactiv-G:** 181 hp / 151 lb-ft torque
- **6-Speed Manual:** Only transmission available
- **Limited Slip Differential:** Standard equipment

## Market Impact & Collectibility

The 30th Anniversary Edition sold out quickly, with many dealers commanding premium prices. The combination of low production numbers, exclusive features, and the significance of the anniversary made it an instant collectible.

**Current Market Values (2024):**
- Average condition: $38,000-$42,000
- Excellent condition: $45,000-$50,000
- Concours/low mileage: $52,000+

## What Makes It Special

The 30th Anniversary Edition represents the pinnacle of modern MX-5 development. The Racing Orange paint, developed specifically for this model, perfectly captures the spirit of classic sports car racing liveries. The Rays wheels not only look fantastic but also provide real performance benefits through weight reduction.

The interior strikes a perfect balance between luxury and sportiness, with the tan leather providing a premium feel while the orange accents remind you that this is a special, limited edition. The numbered production plaque adds to the exclusivity.

## Owner Experience

Owners consistently praise the 30th Anniversary Edition for its combination of daily usability and weekend fun. The enhanced suspension provides better handling without compromising comfort, while the more powerful engine offers improved acceleration and highway performance.

The exclusivity factor cannot be overstated - spotting another 30th Anniversary Edition on the road is a rare occurrence, making ownership feel truly special.

## Legacy

The 30th Anniversary Edition serves as a bridge between the MX-5's storied past and its future. It incorporates lessons learned from three decades of development while pointing toward the continued evolution of the platform. For many, it represents the ultimate expression of the ND generation's capabilities.
